   What is this data pack?

Lipatant's Artefacts is a data/resource pack adding artefacts to Minecraft Java.

These artefacts are special items that give different advantages.

Some grant bonuses that benefit certain playstyles, while others simply offer a little extra content.

The goal behind this pack is to keep to the Minecraft spirit without making the vanilla content obsolete.

   Artefact rarities

Crafted artefacts are consumables that can easily be obtained via crafting, or found as Common artefacts.

Milk Chocolate Bar

Common artefacts are consumables that can be obtained in a large quantity.

Flask of Glowing Glowing (00:10)

Uncommon artefacts are easily obtainable low-impact items.

Swamp Camouflage Tunic +1 Armor Poison Immunity

Rare artefacts artefacts are high-impact items that can be hard to obtain.

Drilling Pickaxe 2 Attack Damage 1.2 Attack Speed +30 Mining Efficiency

Epic artefacts are high-impact end-game items.

Charm of the Endermite -50% Scale -100% Armor Toughness
